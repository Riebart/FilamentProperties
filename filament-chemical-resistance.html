Chemical

Rating Scale (Prusament A–D)

A
Resists very well. No melt, absorption, or swelling after 1-week immersion (<1% change in weight/dimensions).
B
Resists moderately. Slight swelling or weight change (2–3%).
C
Resists poorly. Significant swelling or change (4–5%) with prolonged exposure.
D
Does not resist. Severe change (>5%) or complete destruction of the material.
